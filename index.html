About

Immanuel Portus

"Started as a wannabe aeronautical engineer. Ended up directing for Formula 1 before graduation. The work has always moved faster than the resume."

Immanuel Portus is a Los Angeles-based director with over 2 billion views across global campaigns for Fortnite, Porsche, Fendi, and Oracle Red Bull Racing. In 2023, he became the youngest and first Filipino director in F1 history, directing Oracle Red Bull Racing's campaign at the inaugural Las Vegas Grand Prix — before graduating from Loyola Marymount University's School of Film and Television.

His credits include a Miami Heat commercial screened at the NBA Playoffs, the official film for the 28th SAG Awards, a seven-episode YouTube Premium docuseries, and music videos for Grammy-nominated artists on Warner Music and Insomniac Records. He has been featured in AP News, NBC News, and Speed Magazine.
